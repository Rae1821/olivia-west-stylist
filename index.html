What I do

The Assessment

First you'll fill out a style questionaire to give Olivia (that's me!) an idea of both your current style and your dream style + get an idea of what your day to day lifestyle consists of. This will help me to make the best choices for you!

The Plan

After the questinaire you'll meet with Olivia to discuss your goals in more detail. After the meeting Olivia will put together a personalized style plan. It will include outfit ideas, links to stores with items from your ideas, and much more!

The Box

After reviewing the style plan, Olivia will ship you your first style box that will include items from the plan along with a full description on how to wear them and style them with the existing items you already have.
