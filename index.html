How It Works

Are you ready to feel more stylish and confident in your clothes? I'm here to help! We have a simple, step-by-step process to help you achieve the style and look you dream of!

Step One

First you'll fill out a style questionaire to give Olivia (that's me!) an idea of both your current style and your dream style + get an idea of what your day to day lifestyle consists of. This will help me to make the best choices for you!

Step Two

Next you'll meet with Olivia for a FREE style consultation! This will either be in person or over a Zoom call and will last about 30 minutes. We'll discuss your style questionaire and I'll get to know more about you and your style.

Step Three

After your meeting with Olivia she will write up a personalized "style plan" just for you! It will include outfit ideas, links to stores with items from your outfit ideas section and much more! She will put together and send you your first style box with 5 items she feels will meet your style goals. Shipping & returns are always free!

Step Four

Once you receive your box you will chat with Olivia again to dicuss the fit of the items, how to wear them, and get any questions you might have answered. She will find you up to 5 additional items if you need for free. Our goal is to give you the style of your dreams!
